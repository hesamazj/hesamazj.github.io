About Me

Hello! I am a third year PhD student at Alfred E. Mann Department of Biomedical Engineering, USC. I did my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, Iran and my M.Sc. in BME at USC. I work under supervision of Prof. Valero-Cuevas.

Research

My research areas include Biomedical Data Analysis, Bio-inspired Robotics, and Machine Learning.
